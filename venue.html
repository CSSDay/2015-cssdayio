Hotel Accomodations

If you're traveling to the event, we have some location choices for you to condider...

The Homewood Suites and the Courtyard Phoenix / Chander are across the street to Infusionsoft where the event is held. The benefit here is proximity to the event. The downside is that there are no restaurants within walking distance. However there are plenty of restuarants within two minutes driving.

Another choice for hotels would be the Downtown Chandler District which is about 5 miles from the venue. There are lots of hotel choices and restaurantes within walking distance of each other including Arizona's famous craft beer company and restaurant SanTan Brewing.

Each of the previous choices is about 25 minutes from the airport. If you wish to stay closer to the airport (and to drive about 20 miles to the event), we suggest the Mill Ave. District in Tempe. There are plenty of great hotels there too and tons of restaurants and night-life (way to many to mention).
